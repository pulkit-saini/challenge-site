Challenge Summary

Screen Time & Productivity Challenge

Duration: 15th Nov 2024 to 1st Jan 2025

Objective :

Stay focused, reduce distractions, and improve daily productivity. Keep your scores as low as possible and build better habits!

How it Works:
  • Each day, participants track their screen time and to-do list completion.
  • A 0 means you successfully met the goal, and a 1 means you didn't.
  • Penalties are applied based on incomplete tasks and exceeding the screen time limit.
Rules:
  1. Daily Screen Time: Your total mobile screen time must be less than 5 hours each day.
  2. Daily To-Do List: Complete all the tasks on your to-do list, prepared the night before.
Scoring System:
  • If you complete both tasks (screen time < 5 hours and finish your to-do list), you score 0 for the day.
  • If you fail any one or both tasks, you score 1 for the day.
Penalty:
  • At the end of the challenge (1st January 2025), the person with the highest total score will have to pay ₹200 to the participant with the next lower score.
  • If you fail to keep your screen time under 5 hours, you must pay ₹5 for exceeding the limit.
  • For every incomplete task on your to-do list, you must pay ₹5 per task at the end of the day.
Weekly Off:
  • Each participant gets 1 day off per week, which can be chosen as per their preference.
  • If the weekly off is not used, it will roll over to the next week as an additional off.
